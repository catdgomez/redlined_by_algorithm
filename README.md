# redlined_by_algorithm
This project analyzes Home Mortgage Disclosure Act data to detect potential discrimination by race and other protected classes. 

### Goal: 
Used logistic regression to predict mortgage loan approval outcomes and identify whether race and other protected classes remained statistically significant predictors of denial even after controlling for legitimate financial factors. 

### What is HMDA? and why?: 
HMDA requires mortgage lenders to publicly report data on every loan application they receive: who applied, what they look like demographically, and whether they got approved or denied. If lenders have to disclose their decisions, discriminatory patterns become visible and actionable.

#### Stretch goal: 
Map those findings to the fair lending laws that govern those decisions. Equal Credit Opportunity Act (ECOA), Reg B, (i.e. Regulation B which is the Federal Reserve's rule that spells out exactly how lenders must comply with ECOA), and the Fair Housing Act. I will also apply emerging federal guidance on algorithmic bias and AI model risk.
